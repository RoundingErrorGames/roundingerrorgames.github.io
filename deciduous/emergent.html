Deciduous: Emergent

Coming Spring 2025 for Android

Explore evolution from outside time.

In Deciduous: Emergent, you guide the evolution of a simple microbial ecosystem. Your goal is to develop self-stabilizing ecosystems, by selecting the right mix of species and guiding the evolution of those species. The twist is, you sit just outside of time. As you pick your interventions, you also get to investigate alternate parallel timelines.
