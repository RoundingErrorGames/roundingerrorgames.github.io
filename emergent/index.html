Deciduous: Emergent

Coming Spring 2025 for Android

Explore evolution from outside time.

In Deciduous: Emergent, you guide the evolution of a simple microbial ecosystem. Over time, your ecosystem and its critters evolve naturally. You sit just outside of time. As you apply artificial interventions and select critters, you investigate alternate parallel timelines.

Your goal is to develop self-stabilizing ecosystems, by selecting the right mix of species and guiding the evolution of those species.
